      About this project

      AI Career Success Advisor pairs a Flask backend with trained machine learning models to turn academic and career signals into a clear, structured read on where a student stands — and exactly what to work on next. This interface is the dashboard layer: every slider, dropdown and counter above maps one-to-one to a feature the models were trained on.
